Hecho En Mexico.

El Jarrito mission is to showcase and preserve the rich cultural heritage of Indigenous people in what is now known as Mexico. El Jarrito empowers artisans from Oaxaca, Mexico who create Indigenous and Mexican crafts for everyone. Founded by a Chatino family who are indigenous to Cieneguilla, Oaxaca, Mexico.
